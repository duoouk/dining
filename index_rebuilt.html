About Us

The Private Dining Club is Kent’s premier luxury outdoor catering company. We specialize in creating bespoke dining experiences in outdoor venues and private gardens, combining exceptional cuisine with elegant presentation and flawless service.

Whether it’s an intimate birthday gathering, a grand wedding celebration, or a stylish corporate event, our expert team delivers tailored menus using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From rustic BBQs and grazing tables to five-star private chef banquets, every detail is carefully curated to reflect your style.
